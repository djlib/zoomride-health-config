Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2026

D3M iCabbi Extension respects your privacy.

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

What the extension does

D3M iCabbi Extension runs locally in your browser to enhance the iCabbi Operator View by:

  • Modifying page layout and visuals
  • Displaying contextual labels, tooltips, and driver schedule information
  • Reading configuration data provided by the fleet administrator

Data collection

  • No personal data is collected
  • No authentication credentials are accessed
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No tracking or analytics are used

Data processing

All processing happens locally in the user’s browser on the following site only: 

https://console4.icabbi.us/

Third-party services

The extension does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated if the extension functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact the extension administrator.
